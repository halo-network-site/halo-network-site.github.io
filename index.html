More control of your data than you’ve ever had.

Unparalleled privacy, invulnerable to data-loss, and topped off with an indisputable audit trail. Project Halo provides a system where you can store, share, and interact with your most sensitive data the way you should - With full certainty of who gets to see it and where it goes.

Introducing the Halo Box

We’ve completely redesigned what it means to store your own data. By using a combination of Blockchain and Trusted Execution Environments (TEEs), the Halo.Box allows you to track every action performed on your data and redefines the way you let apps interact with your data. Photos, documents, passwords, cryptocurrency, you name it. Built with 3 layers of back-up, your data is safe and recoverable even if your Halo.Box gets destroyed.

The Halo Network

The Halo.Network is a platform where you can build, browse, and subscribe to applications that follow the Halo.Protocol. Any app built using the Halo.Protocol becomes instantly GDPR compliant and satisfies even the most restrictive privacy regulations. From VPNs to digital inheritance services, your user data is always stored with you.
